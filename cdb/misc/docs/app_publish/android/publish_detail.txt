App name
--------
Masjid Dashboard

Short description
-----------------
Your masjid's prayer and iqamah time


Full description
----------------
Masjid Dashboard is a central, customizable app for prayer and iqamah times.
Masjid that do not have a mobile app can be register by visiting https://www.masjiddashboard.com.
Once your masjid is setup Masjid Dashboard will show your masjid's prayer and iqamah times.
If you choose to it will notify at Azan and iqamah times.
